Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. Known for its sleek designs, powerful engines, and racing heritage, Ferrari has become synonymous with high performance and prestige. The company has a storied history in motorsport, particularly Formula One racing, where it has garnered numerous championships and accolades. In addition to its racing success, Ferrari produces exclusive road cars that are coveted by enthusiasts around the world for their combination of performance, craftsmanship, and status symbol. With a focus on innovation and cutting-edge technology, Ferrari continues to push the boundaries of automotive excellence while maintaining its iconic brand image.
